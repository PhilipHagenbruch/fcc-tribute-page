Jyotsna Jyoti Bhatt

Life

6 March 1940
Bhatt was born in Mandvi, Kutch State.
1958
She joins the Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda to study sculpture.
1960
At the Brooklyn Museum Art School, she continues her studies under Jolyon Hofstedt.
1972
After her return to India, she joined the Maharaja Sayajirao University of Baroda as a professor.
2002
She retires as the Head of the Department of Ceramics.
11 July 2020
Bhatt dies two days after suffering a stroke.
